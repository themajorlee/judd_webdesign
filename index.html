MARFA

Donald Judd is a twentieth-century American artist and designer. He is famous for creating free-standing structures and objects that primarily reside in the west Texas art oasis called Marfa. Known for his literal and often misnomered “minimalist” designs, Judd remains one of the most influential artists of his time.
